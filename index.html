My story

Alexandra Parrales

I immigrated from Perú to the United States when I was turning 12, and found my passion for beauty at 15. Today, at 22, I am a licensed esthetician with three years of experience — and every session I do is a reflection of that passion.

My desire with AP Beauty Lab is to help women feel more confident in their bodies and skin. As a bilingual professional, I am proud to connect with a diverse range of clients and create a comfortable, personalized experience for everyone.

I am certified in Lymphatic Drainage massage and have been fully licensed since 2023. I strive for excellence in every detail — so that each client leaves feeling confident, refreshed, and truly cared for.
